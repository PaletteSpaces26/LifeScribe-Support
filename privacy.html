Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. At LifeScribe, we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of one of our services.

1. Data Storage & Sync

LifeScribe is designed to be a private, offline-first application that syncs via Apple's iCloud services.

All your journal entries, mood logs, and goals are stored locally on your device and synchronized to your private iCloud container. We (the developers of LifeScribe) do not have access to your data. It is encrypted by Apple and accessible only by you.

2. Health Data

With your explicit permission, LifeScribe interacts with the Apple Health app (HealthKit) to display your steps, sleep analysis, and water intake.

  • This data is read directly from your device's Health store.
  • It is processed locally on your device to generate daily summaries.
  • It is strictly used for display purposes within the app and is never transmitted to third-party servers or used for marketing.

3. Face ID / Touch ID

LifeScribe uses Local Authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to secure your journal. This authentication happens entirely on-device via the iOS Secure Enclave. We do not receive or store your biometric data.

4. Analytics

LifeScribe does not track you. We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to track your behavior or collect personally identifiable information.
