Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data?

LifeScribe uses iCloud automatically. Ensure you are signed into your Apple ID on all devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your journal entries and mood logs are stored in your private iCloud container. We do not have access to your data.

How do I delete my account?

Since we do not store your data on our servers, simply deleting the app and its iCloud data from your device settings will remove all your information.
